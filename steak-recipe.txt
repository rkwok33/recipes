Ingredients:
 - Steak
 - Salt
 - Pepper
 - Canola Oil

Directions:
 1. Add Canola Oil to steak and sprinkle salt and pepper on top. Pat in spice with 
	hands
 
 2. Heat pan to high heat and pour canola oil. Bring to a smokey state
 
 3. Place Prepped steak on pan and sear each side to desired rareness
